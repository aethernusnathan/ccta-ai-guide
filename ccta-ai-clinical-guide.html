HeartFlow
FFRCT · Plaque Analysis · PCI Planner
FDACE
Cleared — US, EU, UK, Japan, Canada

FFRCT: FDA cleared Nov 2014. 3D CFD model. 600+ publications. NXT trial AUC 0.90, PACIFIC AUC 0.94 per-vessel. NICE-recommended in UK. FISH&CHIPS n=90,553 NHS patients.

Plaque Analysis: First generation FDA cleared Oct 2022. Next Gen Plaque Analysis cleared Sep 2025 (updated algorithm, expanded nomogram, 3D visualization). 95% agreement with IVUS (REVEALPLAQUE). Cigna coverage from Oct 2025.

PCI Planner: Virtual stent simulation. CE + FDA cleared.

No advisory relationship between author and HeartFlow.

Cleerly
LABS · ISCHEMIA · COMPARE
FDA
Cleared — US only. No CE mark for ISCHEMIA.

LABS v1.0: FDA cleared 2020. LABS v2.0: FDA cleared Mar 7, 2025 (K242338).

ISCHEMIA module: Provides vessel-level ischemia probability (AI-QCTISCHEMIA). Per SCCT-SCAI-ACC 2026, explicitly classified as outside CT-FFR scope — it is not a continuous FFR value and cannot report post-stenosis or delta CT-FFR. Not interchangeable with CT-FFR ≤0.80 threshold.

COMPARE: Serial longitudinal plaque monitoring module — no equivalent from other cleared vendors.

In CREDENCE (Cleerly-sponsored): ISCHEMIA AUC 0.86 vs HeartFlow 0.76 per-vessel. In independent PACIFIC dataset: Cleerly 0.91 vs HeartFlow 0.90 per-patient — no significant difference.

No advisory relationship between author and Cleerly.

Keya Medical
DEEPVESSEL FFR
FDACENMPAHSA
DEEPVESSEL FFR: US (Apr 2022) · EU (CE Aug 2018) · China NMPA (Jan 2020) · Singapore HSA

DEEPVESSEL FFR: Deep learning model trained on CCTA + invasive FFR. FDA cleared April 2022, CE certified August 2018, NMPA January 2020, HSA-certified (Singapore). Validated in ADAPT multicenter trial (10 sites US + EU). Published in TARGET RCT (vs functional testing in intermediate stenosis).

Plaque module: Keya has announced a plaque analysis product. A separate FDA clearance for a plaque module has not been independently confirmed in public FDA databases at time of writing. Readers should verify current clearance status directly with Keya Medical or the FDA 510(k) database.

⚠ Author holds an advisory relationship with Keya Medical. Readers should weigh this when evaluating information about this vendor. All factual claims are sourced to public records.

Elucid
PlaqueIQ — CT-VH™
FDA
FDA cleared Oct 1, 2024

Plaque composition analysis validated against histological ground truth (CT-VH™). First FDA-cleared tool to classify LRNC (lipid-rich necrotic core), IPH, and fibrous components based on histology — methodologically distinct from HU-threshold classification. Limited commercial deployment — in beta/limited release as of Q4 2024. Pursuing FFRCT indication (not yet cleared).

No advisory relationship between author and Elucid.

Caristo Diagnostics
CaRi-Plaque · CaRi-Heart
FDACE
CaRi-Plaque: FDA Mar 2025. CaRi-Heart: CE 2021, FDA pending.

Pericoronary fat attenuation index (FAI) — measures coronary inflammation visible even when the artery shows <50% stenosis. Lancet 2024 outcomes data. CPT 0992T/0993T (Category III, Jan 2026). CaRi-Heart FDA clearance pending — verify payer coverage before clinical deployment as Category III codes have lower reimbursement coverage than Category I.

No advisory relationship between author and Caristo.

Artrya
Salix Anatomy · Salix Coronary Plaque
FDA
Anatomy: FDA Mar 2025. Plaque: FDA Aug 21, 2025 (K251837).

Web-based platform (ASX: AYA). Plaque module offers sub-10-minute turnaround without requiring multiple systems. Limited published outcome data — recently cleared. Pursuing Coronary Flow (FFRCT) module — not yet cleared.

No advisory relationship between author and Artrya.

Circle CVI
cvi42 | Plaque
FDACE
FDA cleared Oct 2025. CE marked.

On-premise plaque analysis — data stays within the institution. Integrates with existing cvi42 platform (1,700+ hospitals, 90 countries). Enables full CPT 75577 physician read fee to be retained in-house. Plaque characterization covers calcified, NCP, and total burden — less granular composition analysis than Cleerly or Elucid (no LAP subtype or histology-based classification). No FFRCT module.

No advisory relationship between author and Circle CVI.

Sponsorship disclosed where known. Independent datasets distinguished from sponsor-funded trials. Weight study quality when drawing conclusions: prospective multicenter RCT > registry > retrospective single-center.
FISH&CHIPS — NHS England
2025 · Nat Med · n=90,553
Largest real-world FFRCT dataset. 27 NHS hospitals before/after FFRCT availability. FFRCT associated with reduced second-line stress tests and ICA; those proceeding to ICA more likely to be revascularized. Observational design — not randomized.
n=90,55327 NHS sitesObservationalHeartFlow FFRCT
PRECISE RCT
2023 · JAMA Cardiol
Prospective RCT. CCTA ± FFRCT vs standard care. Fewer catheterizations without obstructive CAD. Safety equivalent at 1 year. FFRCT used selectively in 39% of CCTAs — not mandated in all patients.

NXT Trial
2014 · JACC · n=254
Prospective multicenter diagnostic accuracy vs invasive FFR. Per-vessel: sensitivity 84%, specificity 86%, AUC 0.90. CCTA alone: 91% sensitivity, 41% specificity. Foundational HeartFlow study underpinning regulatory approvals.
Sens 84%Spec 86%AUC 0.90HeartFlow
TARGET RCT — DEEPVESSEL FFR
2023 · Circulation
RCT comparing DEEPVESSEL FFR-guided management vs functional testing in intermediate stenosis. DVFFR arm: lower ICA use, higher revascularization rate. Published evidence for Keya DEEPVESSEL FFR in an RCT setting.
RCTKeya DEEPVESSEL FFR↓ unnecessary ICA
CREDENCE — Cleerly vs HeartFlow
2024 · JACC CVI · n=868 vessels
Both platforms analyzed same dataset. Per-vessel: Cleerly ISCHEMIA AUC 0.86 vs HeartFlow FFRCT 0.76 (p<0.001). Cleerly only test prognostic for MACE at 8yr in this cohort.
⚠ Sponsored by Cleerly. HeartFlow algorithm version used was not current. Do not interpret without reading the PACIFIC independent dataset result below.

REVEALPLAQUE — HeartFlow Plaque vs IVUS
2024 · EHJ CV Imaging
95% agreement in total plaque volume between HeartFlow Plaque Analysis and IVUS. Validates CT-based plaque quantification against an intravascular reference standard. Study conducted prior to Next Gen algorithm update (Sep 2025).

DECIDE Registry
2024 · SCCT
Adding AI plaque staging to FFRCT changed management in >50% of cases. LDL reduced 18.7 mg/dL at 90 days. Combined FFRCT + plaque AUC 0.73 vs CCTA alone 0.63 for 7-year MACE (ADVANCE-DK). Registry design — not randomized.

SCCT-SCAI-ACC CT-FFR Consensus
May 2026 · JCCT · doi:10.1016/j.jcct.2026.05.006
CT-FFR recommended for 50–90% stenosis (LOE A, stable chest pain). Not appropriate for LM ≥50%, CTO, stents, <1.8mm vessels. Post-lesional measurement 2cm distal = standard. 7 platforms clinically cleared globally as of May 2026. Cleerly ISCHEMIA outside CT-FFR scope. Spimed-AI and Siemens cFFR research-only.

ACC Scientific Statement on AI-QCPA
Dec 2025 · JACC CVI
First formal ACC consensus on AI plaque analysis. Appropriate for patients with visual plaque on CCTA for risk stratification. Not indicated for all CCTA patients. Cross-vendor reproducibility data still needed.
Appropriate use definedNot all CCTA patients
Nitroglycerin is mandatory for FFRCT submission per SCCT-SCAI-ACC 2026. 400–800 mcg SL immediately pre-scan. Without nitrates, CT-FFR values are systematically higher (falsely reassuring). Hold only if SBP <90 mmHg or severe aortic regurgitation.
