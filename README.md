# CCTA-AI Clinical Guide

**An independent clinical reference for cardiologists evaluating FFRCT and AI coronary plaque analysis vendors.**

Built by [Nathan Qin](https://www.linkedin.com/in/nathanqin) as a personal project. Free. No vendor has reviewed or paid for any content.

→ **[Read the guide](https://aethernusnathan.github.io/ccta-ai-guide)** · [Get update emails](https://aethernusnathan.github.io/ccta-ai-guide#signup)

---

## What this covers

Ten commercially active FFRCT and AI plaque analysis platforms, assessed across five chapters:

| Chapter | Content |
|---|---|
| I — Technology Primer | What FFR<sub>CT</sub> and AI-QCPA actually measure, and why they answer different clinical questions |
| II — Vendor Scorecards | Evidence-ranked strength/weakness analysis for 6 major platforms |
| III — Feature Matrix | 35+ criteria across all 8 FDA-cleared platforms |
| IV — Evidence Review | Key trials with bias disclosures; Cleerly vs HeartFlow head-to-head data |
| V — Clinical Decision Guide | 9 patient scenarios with specific platform recommendations |

**Vendors covered:** HeartFlow · Cleerly · Keya Medical · Elucid · Caristo · Circle CVI · Artrya · Siemens · Shukun · HeartMedi+

## How to contribute

Errors, outdated data, missing vendors, missing studies — all welcome.

**To flag a factual error:**
Open an [issue](https://github.com/aethernusnathan/ccta-ai-guide/issues) with:
- The specific claim that's wrong
- What the correct information is
- Your source (ideally a DOI or public FDA filing)

**To suggest a new study or vendor:**
Same — open an issue with the relevant citation or regulatory filing link.

**Pull requests:**
Welcome for straightforward factual corrections. For interpretive changes (vendor ratings, clinical recommendations), open an issue first so we can discuss.
