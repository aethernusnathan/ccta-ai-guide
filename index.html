What's inside

Five chapters. Evidence-first.

I
Technology Primer
What FFRCT and AI-QCPA measure, how the algorithms differ (3D CFD vs deep learning vs AI-QCT), and why neither is sufficient alone.
II
Vendor Scorecards
Six platforms rated across five dimensions — evidence base, diagnostic accuracy, plaque depth, workflow speed, global reach — with plain-language strengths and weaknesses.
III
Feature Matrix
35+ criteria across all eight cleared platforms: regulatory status, CPT codes, plaque subtypes, deployment model, serial monitoring, PCI planning integration.
IV
Evidence Review & Head-to-Head Data
Key trials with bias disclosures. The Cleerly vs HeartFlow comparison: AUC 0.86 vs 0.76 in CREDENCE (sponsored) vs 0.91 vs 0.90 in PACIFIC (independent). Both results, with context.
V
Clinical Decision Guide
Nine patient scenarios — intermediate stenosis, high calcium, preventive cardiology, serial monitoring, pre-PCI, data sovereignty, practice economics — with specific platform recommendations.
